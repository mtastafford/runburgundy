 How to join our fitness group:
 * Make yourself a (free!) account for athletics tracking at [Runalyze](https://runalyze.com) (open-source running / athletics analytics site);
 * Post some GPS tracked runs/bikes/hikes to your new Runalyze account; 
 * Talk to @mstafford or @aussieninja to prove you're real;
 * Vote on these daily posts to increase their rewards;
 * At the end of each week, the SBD rewards get distributed based on kilometres travelled!
